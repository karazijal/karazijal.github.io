About

I am currently a DPhil (PhD) student at University of Oxford, AIMS CDT, advised by Prof A. Vedaldi, Dr C. Rupprecht, and Dr I. Laina at VGG. I am broadly interested in unsupervised scene understanding, with emphasis on objects.

In previous life, I worked as MLE for OakNorth and Bloomberg. I graduated with MEng in Computer Science from University of Cambridge, supervised by Prof Liò. Feel free to explore further. Apologies, this site is still under construction.
